Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ML Interview Prep collects, uses, and stores information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Study progress, bookmarks, practice history, and self-assessment scores stored locally on your device.
  • Audio recordings you create during practice sessions, stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated in the app.
  • Purchase and subscription information processed by Apple through the App Store.

How We Use Information

  • To provide question practice, bookmarking, mock interviews, and progress tracking.
  • To unlock subscription features after a valid App Store purchase.
  • To restore purchases through Apple's StoreKit and App Store systems.

Data Storage

The app is designed to store core user data locally on your device. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

Subscriptions and Payments

Payments, billing, renewals, and refunds are handled by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details.

Microphone Access

If you use practice recording features, the app requests microphone access to record your spoken answers.

Third-Party Services

Apple may process data related to purchases, subscriptions, device information, and App Store operations under Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • You can deny microphone access in iOS Settings, though some practice features may not work.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@mlinterviewprep.app
